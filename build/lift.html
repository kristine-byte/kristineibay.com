Design & Development

Lift Enhanced

Lift Enhanced is an e-store that sells athletic/athleisure wear, plus virtual personalized workout plans with professionals in the U.S.
Overview

Design an eye catching e-commerce website focused on customers with active lifestyles.

This website was built on Shopify using a theme as a base. The theme was then customized to fit the client's needs. Customizations included, but not limited to, coach pages and sections, work out plan product pages, email notification updates that added a custom link to the purchased workout plans, and more.
